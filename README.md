# SmartInvest AI
Business Insights into Stock Market and Sentiment Influence on Stock Price

SmartInvest AI is a machine learning–powered application that integrates news sentiment analysis with historical stock market data to generate real-time BUY/SELL recommendations.
The system uses a TF-IDF–based SVM model for sentiment classification, merged with stock indicators to evaluate overall investment signals across top companies such as Apple, Microsoft, Google, Amazon, and Nvidia.

# Business Insights
SmartInvest AI identifies companies with strong public sentiment and correlates their news signals with price trends.
This allows investors to:

Prioritize companies with positive momentum

Detect early sentiment shifts

Benchmark sentiment-driven performance

Identify stocks with negative or volatile sentiment
